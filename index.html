Research Overview

🎯 Dynamic Balance

Novel control algorithms that enable quadrupedal robots to maintain stability on moving skateboard platforms, extending beyond traditional walking gaits.

🧠 AI Learning

Reinforcement learning framework that teaches robots complex skateboarding maneuvers through progressive curriculum learning and reward shaping.

🔄 Real-World Transfer

Successful simulation-to-reality transfer with robust performance across different surface conditions and environmental variations.

⚡ Multi-Modal Control

Seamless transition between walking and skateboarding modes with hierarchical control architecture combining planning and execution.

Key Contributions

📊 Novel Control Framework

First comprehensive approach to quadrupedal robot skateboarding with hierarchical control combining MPC trajectory planning and joint-space torque control.

🎓 Learning Methodology

Progressive curriculum learning approach that systematically teaches complex skateboarding maneuvers from basic balance to advanced tricks.

🔬 Scientific Validation

Extensive experimental validation in both simulation and real-world environments, demonstrating robust performance across conditions.

🌐 Open Research

Complete open-source framework enabling reproducible research and further development in sports robotics applications.
